What Is The Guiding Light?

The guiding light is a revolutionary AI image recogition model trained to help the blind and visually impaired detect potential everyday hazards. We are always working to add more hazards to our model, but right now we have the following classes...

  1. Flammable Signs and Toxic Signs
  2. Stop Signs
  3. Wet Floor Signs
  4. High Voltage Signs
  5. Cars or Buses In Front of the Camera
  6. Nothing/Everyday Environment
More Information on the Guiding Light can be found at our website
